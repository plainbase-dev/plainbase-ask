# Dashboard

The Dashboard is the first thing you see when you log into the admin. It gives you a quick snapshot of what's happening — no settings to configure here, it's purely read-only.

## What you'll see

At the top, four counters give you a high-level view:

- **Conversations** — total number of chat sessions started by visitors
- **Messages** — total individual messages sent across all conversations
- **Tickets** — total support tickets submitted through the widget
- **Documents** — total number of indexed documents in the knowledge base

Below that, a table shows the **20 most recent conversations**, ordered newest first. Each row shows:

- The conversation ID (shortened) — click it to jump to that conversation in the Conversations view
- The visitor's email address, or "anonymous" if they didn't provide one
- When the conversation started
- How many messages it contained

## What it's useful for

Glancing at the dashboard before diving into other sections tells you whether the bot is being used, whether tickets are coming in, and whether your knowledge base has content. If all four counters are zero after setup, something is probably not wired up correctly.
